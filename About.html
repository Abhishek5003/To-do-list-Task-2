᭄ About This Page

  • Welcome to Be Productive, a simple and beautifully designed to-do list application built entirely with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript .

  • This app empowers you to manage your daily tasks with ease—add them, update them, mark them completed, and store your list locally without needing an internet connection.

  • Crafted with love and lots of glowing gradients, this app showcases clean front-end design, responsive layout, dark mode support, and animation-rich UI—all built without any libraries or frameworks.

  • Whether you’re learning front-end development or just trying to stay organized, this app is here to help you stay focused and productive every day.
